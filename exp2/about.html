About Titan Watch Company

Titan Company Limited (Titan) is a leading Indian luxury goods company and a subsidiary of the Tata Group. It was established in 1984 and has since become one of the largest watch manufacturers in India.

Titan offers a wide range of watches catering to different segments of the market, including fashion, sports, formal, and luxury. The company is renowned for its innovative designs, superior craftsmanship, and commitment to quality.

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction and continuous innovation, Titan has earned the trust and loyalty of millions of customers worldwide. The company's dedication to excellence has earned it numerous awards and accolades over the years.

At Titan, we believe that every watch tells a story and represents a unique journey. Our timepieces are not just accessories; they are timeless pieces of art that reflect the wearer's personality and style.

Thank you for choosing Titan Watch Company. We look forward to being a part of your journey and helping you make every moment count.
